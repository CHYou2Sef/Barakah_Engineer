### 1. Choose Your Path
Toggle between **PRO** and **SIMPLE** modes in the top navbar. 
- **PRO**: Strict timings, 5 AM start, high-density focus.
- **SIMPLE**: Flexible blocks, maintenance focus.

### 2. Localization
Use the **EN / AR / FR** buttons in the navbar to switch the entire interface to your preferred language.

### 3. Sync Your School Schedule (AI Brain)
Don't waste time typing your university schedule.
- Click the **AI Sync** (Upload icon) button.
- You can either **paste text** or **upload an image/PDF** of your schedule.
- The Dynamic AI Brain will analyze the document and merge it with your Barakah routines (Prayers, Meals, Workouts).

### 4. Manage Your Missions (CRUD)
- **Add**: Use the blue "Add Task" button.
- **Modify**: Hover over any task and click the **Pencil icon** to edit details, priority, or timing.
- **Delete**: Click the **Trash icon** to remove a task.
- **Priority**: Set tasks to HIGH, MEDIUM, or LOW to see them color-coded.

### 5. Training & Nutrition
- Check the **Hypertrophy Blueprint** section for your daily bodyweight split.
- Follow the meal details provided in the PRO schedule for optimal muscle protein synthesis.

### 6. Prayer & Barakah
- Ensure Location permissions are granted.
- The app automatically calculates prayer times based on your current Tunisian (or worldwide) coordinates.
- Get a new motivational tip daily by clicking the activity pulse icon.
